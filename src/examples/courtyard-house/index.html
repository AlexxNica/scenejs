Cross-Domain Load of a COLLADA House Model from SceneJS.org

This is a larger COLLADA model than other examples, loaded cross-domain from SceneJS.org through a proxy server. It has 149 texture images, so it may take some time to load, depending on your connection speed and how busy the proxy is right now.

Model is courtesy of VaST architecture.

This scene is interactive; to rotate the view, it takes two variables, "yaw" and "pitch", which are injected into the scene graph along with "dist" for the distance of the eye on the Z-axis. Take a close look at the lookAt and rotate nodes, which use these variables, and the invocation of the "render" function near the bottom of this example, which passes them in.
